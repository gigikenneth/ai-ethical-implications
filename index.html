Project Aim and Goals

This project aims to identify best practices and loopholes in existing AI policies across different countries and regions and provide recommendations.

The goals of this project include:

  • Promoting the responsible use of AI by identifying best practices, analyzing existing policies, and providing recommendations for better transparency and accountability.
  • Helping everyday individuals and organizations understand the benefits and impact of AI policies.

The project welcomes contributions and suggestions from interested individuals. If you would like to contribute, please check out the GitHub repository.

I worked on this project with the help of my mentor, Isil during the Open Life Science Fellowship (OLS-7), which provided valuable support and resources to help advance the project's objectives.
