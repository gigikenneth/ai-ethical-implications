Project Aim and Goals

This project aims to explore and highlight the ethical implications of artificial intelligence (AI) in various domains. By examining the potential risks and benefits, we strive to foster a deeper understanding of AI's impact on society and encourage responsible development and deployment of AI technologies. The project is a part of the Open Life Science Fellowship (OLS-7).
