Project Aim and Goals

This project aims to explore and highlight the ethical implications of artificial intelligence (AI) in various domains. By examining the potential risks and benefits, we strive to foster a deeper understanding of AI's impact on society and encourage responsible development and deployment of AI technologies. The project welcomes contributions from interested individuals. If you would like to contribute, please check out the GitHub repository.

I worked on this project during the Open Life Science Fellowship, which provided valuable support and resources to help advance the project's objectives.
